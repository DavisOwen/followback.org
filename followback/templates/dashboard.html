FollowBack Bot

Automatically likes and follows users. Specify which pages best fit your niche and we will find the users to like and follow. Specify maximum number of users to follow and like per day. Unfollows all users not on whitelist at the end of the day. The bot will pause between liking and following users to avoid spammy behavior which will get you banned. Cannot run at asame time as Unfollow Bot

Warning: While FollowBack allows you to follow and like as many people per day as you want, we strongly recommend you do not exceed 800 follows per day and 1200 likes per day. Past this point, you are at risk of getting temporarily banned by Instagram. Start at 400 follows per day and 600 likes per day, then increase by 50 follows per day and 25 likes per day every 2 weeks.

Unfollow Bot

Utility for unfollowing all users not on your whitelist. Specify how many users to unfollow per day. Cannot run at the same time as FollowBack Bot

Warning: While FollowBack allows you to unfollow as many people per day as you want, we strongly recommend you do not exceed 800 unfollows per day. Past this point, you are at risk of getting temporarily banned by Instagram. We recommend you use the same amount of unfollows per day as follows per day in the FollowBack Bot.

Edit Whitelist

Add users to your whitelist. You can manually specify certain users to add to your whitelist. Use this option if you are manually following users while FollowBack Bot is running and do not want FollowBack to unfollow these users. You can also create a whitelist based on all the users you are currently following (recommended for new users).

Warning: Creating a whitelist from all users you are currently following will overwrite your previous whitelist. Use with caution.
